About Us

We started out as a small passionate group that set out to discover profound ties to our ancestors. This culminated into a strong following that we collected a haul of information. Thus, Wahenga was born. Now, we want to help you find your roots.

Mission

Build your Family Tree with ease and immortalize your family in history.

Vision

Your tree and Wahenga’s family records help you discover your unique story. Discover more about your ethnicity and your living relatives.
